## üé¨ Production Notes

### **Visual Guidelines:**
- **Clean UI Focus:** Keep phone/screen clearly visible
- **Smooth Transitions:** Use fade/slide transitions between features
- **Real Data:** Show realistic Indian product names and prices
- **Multi-language:** Highlight language switching capabilities
- **Professional Lighting:** Ensure screen content is clearly readable

### **Audio Guidelines:**
- **Clear Narration:** Professional, enthusiastic but not overly excited
- **Background Music:** Subtle, modern, tech-focused
- **Sound Effects:** Minimal - let the app functionality speak
- **Pace:** Steady but engaging, allowing viewers to follow along

### **Technical Setup:**
- **Screen Recording:** Use high-quality screen recording (1080p minimum)
- **Demo Data:** Pre-load with realistic Indian products and transactions
- **Stable Internet:** Ensure AI features work smoothly during recording
- **Backup Plans:** Have screenshots ready in case of API issues

### **Key Messages to Emphasize:**
1. **AI-Powered:** Advanced technology made simple
2. **India-Focused:** Built for Indian shopkeepers and customers
3. **Complete Solution:** Not just an app, but a business transformation
4. **Multilingual:** Accessible to diverse Indian population
5. **Real-World Ready:** Practical, immediately usable solution
